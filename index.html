Cyber Salamat

The Digital Safety Campaign offers advice and guidance to stay secure online to 500 million new users as India continues to rely on technology for Covid-19 vaccinations, homeschooling, and more online payments.


As digital technologies become further integrated into the everyday lives of Indians, new internet users are potentially exposed to greater risks. However, the risks and benefits of internet access go hand in hand. The need of the hour, therefore, is to support these new users to minimise the risks without limiting their digital participation and their capacity to derive the full benefits of internet access.
With our Digital Safety Campaign, we aim to:
  • Educate communities on digital safety by using creative messaging on digital platforms
  • Introduce Grassroots Comics as a tool to share personal experiences of internet users
  • Promote digital equity by providing accessible knowledge of internet basics.
